The effective and practical way to follow interna-
tional market indicators: Tekno Kitap!
This small yet well-equipped book, which explains the methods of reading, interpreting, and
tracking important market indicators of leading
players in the world economy, primarily the USA,
takes its reader on a quick world tour without making their head spin. Dr. Hakan Özerol, promising
active learning by presenting theoretical knowledge
accompanied by practical data available on the
internet, reconstructs expectations towards academic books with this innovative approach.
