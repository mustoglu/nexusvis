Customers now prefer companies that make
them feel close, appeal to their emotions, understand them, and offer them personalized solutions.
Whether companies design it or not, customers
experience interactions with them, and based on
these experiences, they make purchasing decisions. In this book, we will explore the principles of
customer experience management, best practices,
and how to implement them to open the door to
creating unique experiences for customers.
