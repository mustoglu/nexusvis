The book “If Superman Were Turkish, His Mother
Would Tie His Cape,” which was fi rst published in
2010 and has earned its place among Ahmet Şerif
İzgören’s most beloved works, has been reissued.
The content has been reviewed and updated, and
the title has been changed to “If Superman Were
Turkish.” İzgören addresses the reader on topics
such as entrepreneurship, business quality, honesty, patriotism, and tolerance, incorporating both his
memories and observations into the book.
