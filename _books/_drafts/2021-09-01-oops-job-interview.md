“Uh-oh! The Job Interview” not only aims to improve your performance in job interviews but also
targets reaching higher professional standards.
It tells you what you can do in challenging situations you may encounter in your professional life
and how to identify the right job for you. All of this
is presented with the most sincere and witty approach you can read.
In this book, which is a product of the vast experience of Hakan Yaman and Ahmet Şerif İzgören,
you will also fi nd 180 job interview questions and
the most accurate answers that can be given to
them.
